Interested in joining us?

We are a friendly and sociable bunch who also like going for a drink or dinner together. We practice in Honor Oak on Monday evenings.

If you want to try morris dancing, we are offering a taster session on Monday, 16th September '24. No experience of morris is necessary.

We are looking for adults with a decent level of fitness, good sense of timing and music, who want to dance out with us in public. Once you have had a go, membership to the side will be by invitation.

Get in touch if you are interested!

Book us for events!

We dance at many local events, including festivals, private parties, or just outside all manner of pubs. We're most active in South East London, around the borough of Lewisham, but will travel further afield to spread the morris joy.

If you would like to book us for your event, email us and we'll work out the details together.
