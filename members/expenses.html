Process for Claiming Expenses

As agreed in the AGM 23rd September 2024

All expenses to be agreed in advance & proceed as follows:

  1. Present your case for spending money to the side during practice or on WhatsApp:
    1. Item(s) for purchasing
    2. Method for sourcing (prices from 3 different suppliers comparing unit cost)
    3. Extent of cost for the amount / number required
    4. Reason for purchase
  2. To be agreed in general by the side
  3. Purchase item(s) and source receipt(s)
  4. Claim money back from treasurer
  5. Treasurer refunds the full amount
