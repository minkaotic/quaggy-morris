Walking on

Somewhere in the sidelines, line up in the positions you will dance in, but odds facing forward and evens facing backward. Then everyone follows 1 in a line to walk on. Walk around at least once so everybody is in place. Once 1 shouts "Set up!", everyone takes their position, usually facing the musician.
